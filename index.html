Let a, b ∈ ℤ, not both zero. The greatest common divisor of a and, b, denoted gcd(a, b), is the largest positive integer d such that:

  1. d|a (d divides a) and d|b, and
  2. For any other integer c such that c|a and c|b, we have c ≤ d.
