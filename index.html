A prime number is a natural number greater than 1 that is not a product of two smaller numbers. A natural number greater than 1 that is not prime is called a composite number.
A primalty test is algorithm for determining whether an input number is prime. Enter an integer greater than 1 to check whether it is prime using the trial division test.

n primality

The Fundamental theorem of arithmetic is a theorem of number theory. The theorem says that every positive integer greather than 1 can be written as a product of prime numbers. Moreover, this prime factorization is unique, up to the order of the factors.
